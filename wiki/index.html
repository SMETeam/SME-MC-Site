Economy Info

Ah yes, the most confusing part of the server.. well at least for me..
You can earn money a LOT of ways; This includes Killing Mobs and Players, Orders, Auction, Season Pass, and Shop.

Mobs

Depending on the entity it is, can drop different ammounts. Now we will not tell you here so you have to figure it out your self.

Orders

This is one way you can earn money very fast! If a player is desperately looking for a item usually they tend to put out some orders! To check simply run 
"/orders" (more info on the /help command)
to see the list of current orders! Then simply deliver the item that is needed by the player and boom! You got a cash out!

Auction

Got your self a neat item? Maybe even two of them? Well, selling it may be a good option! Not on the normal shop but on Auction! The Auction is a place where players can sell their items for what ever they want! Ore, Bags, Swords, you name it! You can sell EVERYTHING here! Simply run 
"/ah" or to sell '/ah sell [ammount] [price]" (more info on the /help command)
and you are ready to start selling/purchasing!.

Shop

The shop, the main way to earn money. The shop can sell MOST items, even ones not for sale! But at a catch.. if you sell a Diamond for example and it gives 500$, after selling that diamond the price will go down.. Why? To prevent inflation of the market if someone where to buy a bunch of items. To start using the shop just run
"/shop" or to sell '/sellgui" (more info on the /help command)
