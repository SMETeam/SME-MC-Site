Boss Info

Bosses are powerful mobs that spawn in the world and drop unique loot. They have different levels, abilities, and spawn chances. Learning about them can help you prepare for battles and know what to expect when you encounter one. They spawn 1/600 mobs in the world, so they can be pretty common but also very dangerous! On weekends they have a 2x increase in spawn rate, so keep an eye out!

Dimensions Info

Survival isn't the survival you're expecting.. This server has a semi-modded experience!

Overworld

The main world, filled with custom generation, secret dungeons, and more!

Nether

Fast travel routes, but pretty dangerous.. many players tend to use the nether for hidden bases or to find quartz which gives some good cash!

End

Ender mobs and a respawning dragon. Prepare before heading in and watch for the void..

Aether

A custom sky world, great for traveling, ores, and also for farms!

Economy Info

Ah yes, money. Survival has a lot of ways to make cash, like mobs, orders, auction, season pass, shop, fishing, maybe spending on the Snubby's shop!

Mobs and Players

Some mobs and players can drop money when you kill them. Different things can pay different amounts, so you will have to figure out what is worth farming.

Orders

Orders are items that other players are looking for. If you have the item they need, deliver it and get paid.

/orders

Auction

Got something good but don't want to sell it to the normal shop? Put it on auction and let another player buy it.

/ah /ah sell [amount] [price]

Shop

The shop is the main way to sell items. Prices can go down after people sell a lot of something, so don't expect every item to stay the same forever.

/shop /sellgui

Fishing

Fishing is a chill way to make extra cash. Better and rarer fish can sell for more, so grab a rod and find some water.

Rotating Snubby's Shop

Snubby's shop has a rotating selection of items. Some stuff is rare and stock is limited, so check it before everything good is gone.

One Block

This page is still being worked on.

One Block starts you on one block and you slowly build out from there. For now, make a safe platform, save your better drops, and don't throw away stuff you might need later.

Duels

This page is still being worked on.

Duels is where you can fight other players in quick PvP matches. There are different modes and kits, so check what you are joining before running in.

Bed Wars

This page is still being worked on.

Bed Wars is about protecting your bed while trying to break everyone else's. Save your resources, upgrade when you can, and please don't leave the bed wide open.

Murder Mystery

This page is still being worked on.

Murder Mystery is about figuring out who the murderer is before it is too late. If you are innocent, stay aware. If you are detective, pay attention before shooting.
